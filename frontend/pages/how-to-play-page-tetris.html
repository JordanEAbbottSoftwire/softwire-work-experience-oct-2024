How to play tetris

Objective

The objective of the game is to use the pieces to create as many complete horizontal lines of blocks as possible.

When a line is completed, it disappears, and the blocks placed above fall one rank.

Controls

To control the tetrominoes, players can use the arrow keys on the keyboard:

  • ← Move the tetromino left
  • → Move the tetromino right
  • ↓ Soft drop the tetromino
  • ↑ Hard drop the tetromino
  • Spacebar – Rotate the tetromino

    • GamePlay Basics

    As Tetriminos fall from the top of the screen, use the controls to guide and rotate them to fit into the empty spaces.

    Clear as many lines as possible to earn points and prevent blocks from reaching the top.

    Game gradually increases in speed, making it more challanging to place pieces accurately.
